About the studio

A salon built around you

Strand Hair Studio opened in 2015 with one simple mission - to give every client an experience they actually look forward to. No rushing, no hard sells, just great hair and a genuinely relaxing visit.

Our team of experienced stylists bring creativity and precision to every appointment. Whether it's your first visit or your fiftieth, you'll always leave feeling looked after.
